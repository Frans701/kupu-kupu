Information


Thank you for your visit.

We dedicate ourselves to providing the best quality of specimens as well as knowledge to all enthusiasts all around the world. Your needs and curiosities are our main priority.

This website is managed and run by Detani brothers. Our father is an expert who has been studying both lepidoptera and coleoptera of Indonesia since late 1970s up until now. He was the first one who came in contact with H. leucippe of Peleng, which we are always proud of.

How to Order

Orders can be made in two ways:
I. Via Website’s “Cart” system, or
II. Order via Email

I. Via Website’s “Cart” system
  1. Please register yourself first before start shopping. (Even if you do not register, you can still see the products, but unable to place an order)
  2. Please select the species/item and select or input your desired quantity, then click “Add to cart”
  3. To check out, please click on “My Cart” and select “Place order”
    • Please note that the minimum order is JPY3000 or around US$30, if the total amount of items in your cart is less than the specified amount, you will be able to place an order.
  4. After you place an order, we will send you an invoice to your registered email address, along with payment instruction. Please wait for us at least within 24 hours to contact you, because we need to confirm the availability of the stocks – We update the item availability daily. In case the item is not available or has been ordered by another buyer before you, we will notify you when sending the invoice. We may offer you alternatives via email.
